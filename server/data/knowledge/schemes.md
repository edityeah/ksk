# Skilling Schemes Covered by KSK

KSK is scheme-agnostic. The verification architecture works for any scheme; only the payment trigger configuration differs.

## Schemes on SIDH

### PMKVY (Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana)
India's biggest short-term training scheme. 2-3 month courses. ~1.64 crore trained across 4 versions since 2015. Budget ~₹2,800 Cr (composite pool). Payment per certified trainee, moving to skill vouchers tied to outcomes in PMKVY 5.0.

### NAPS (National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme)
On-the-job training inside real companies for 6-12 months. 51+ lakh apprentices registered. Government pays 25% of monthly stipend (max ₹1,500) directly to the apprentice.

### JSS (Jan Shikshan Sansthan)
Non-formal training for low/no-literacy adults — rural women, slum dwellers, daily wage workers. 289 centres. 34 lakh trained.

### PM Vishwakarma
Training for 18 traditional craft communities — carpenters, potters, blacksmiths, tailors, goldsmiths. Toolkit + credit access. ₹13,000 Cr over 5 years.

### PM VIKAS
Skilling for officially classified minority communities. ₹303 Cr. Converges data with SIDH.

### Skill Impact Bond
Private outcome-based funding. Investors fund training upfront and are repaid only when outcomes are independently verified. 50,000 trainees, 60% women. ₹120 Cr ($14.4M). Most rigorous model.

## Schemes NOT on SIDH

### DDU-GKY (Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana)
Rural Development Ministry's scheme. Stricter than PMKVY — training partners only get full payment when trainees are placed AND retained. Payment in tranches: enrolment, certification, placement, 3-month and 6-month retention. ~17.51 lakh trained. ₹750 Cr.

### PM SETU
Upgrading 3,000+ government-run ITIs. ₹6,140 Cr. World Bank and ADB co-funding. Institutional grants to ITIs.

### SAMARTH (Textiles)
Textile Ministry scheme. 4.58 lakh trained, 3.56 lakh placed in FY25-26. ₹330 Cr.

### DAY-NULM
Urban poverty reduction skilling. ₹536 Cr. Self-help-group linked.

### PM-DAKSH
Training for SC and OBC communities. Ministry of Social Justice. 1.87 lakh trained, 73,000 placed.

### FutureSkills Prime (MeitY + NASSCOM)
Digital and AI skills — cloud, cybersecurity, big data, IoT, blockchain. 15.78 lakh beneficiaries.

## How KSK adapts

Metric definitions are configuration, not hard-code. If a scheme defines retention as 6 months instead of 3, KSK changes a parameter. If it adds earnings growth as a payment trigger, KSK adds that data flow. The verification infrastructure (trainee check-in, employer confirmation, multi-signal triangulation) works regardless of which specific outcomes are being measured.
